About City Guard+

Safe Smarter Cities For Everyone

City Guard+ is an AI-powered urban safety system designed to make cities smarter and safer.
We bring together real-time monitoring, intelligent alerts, and seamless coordination — all in one platform.
Our core strength lies in six smart technologies: Smart Grid Response, AI Watchtower, Safe Path Navigator, CrowdQ System, Accessibility, and InclusiveAccess+ — each built to ensure fast, inclusive, and reliable safety support. Powering these are three advanced systems — AI Planner, Integrated Network, and City Alert Types — working together to predict risks, connect departments, and deliver alerts instantly.
City Guard+ isn’t just security — it’s a city’s intelligent guardian, protecting lives with speed, clarity, and care.
